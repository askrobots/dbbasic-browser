Waiting for payment…

The QR encodes a standard EIP-681 payment link. Some wallets pre-fill only the address and ignore the amount — confirm the amount shown above before sending. Wallet payments settle on-chain and you pay the network's own gas (a few cents on Base). Apple Pay and card payments fund your wallet balance instead, because card rails carry a fixed ~$0.30 fee that makes per-nickel charges uneconomical — fund once, then drawing from the balance costs nothing to authorize.

Prototype — buttons simulate the handoff; nothing is charged.
